Privacy Policy

Your data is secure in our arena. Transparency is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, book a turf, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name, username, and password.
  • Contact Data: Email address and phone number.
  • Financial Data: Payment details (processed securely via third-party gateways).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website and services.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to facilitate your bookings, improve our platform, and communicate with you. Specifically:

Service Execution

Processing bookings and managing your dashboard.

Communication

Sending booking confirmations and support updates.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data but cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. These are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and login status. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of the site may not function properly.
